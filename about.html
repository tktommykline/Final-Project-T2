About Us


Our mission: to bring the beauty of nature indoors to create more comfortable, attractive, and productive places to live and work.


We at Athenia’s Garden believe that plants are an intimate part of our lives—we live with them and love them every day— and we want to share this lifestyle with you by carefully curating kits with hardy plants that will ease your anxiety about greenery care. With the help of our growers and horticulturists, we can help you strengthen your relationship with plants and reconnect with nature.
